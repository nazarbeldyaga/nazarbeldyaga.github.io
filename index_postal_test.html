Skyrim Dragon's Review

Today I will tell you about the dragons of Skyrim.

Basic Information

Dragons are intelligent lizards, children of Akatosh, who live in Nirn. When they appeared in Nirn is unknown, but they may have been the first intelligent species on the planet. The homeland of dragons is considered to be the continent of Akavir, from where they subsequently migrated and spread throughout Tamriel. For a long time, these creatures were considered either extinct or simply flew away to an unknown location, until the events of The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim. According to the plot of this game, it becomes known that most of the dragons were killed, but since only the Dragonborn can finally destroy the dragon, Alduin, the firstborn of Akatosh, who returned "from beyond the world", gradually begins to bring them back to life. During the game, you can watch as Alduin personally resurrects his relative (Sahloknir) in the dragon mound.

A Common Dragon

The weakest type of dragon. According to history, these dragons appeared on Akavir, where the Tsaesci tried to destroy them. During the war between these two races, almost all the dragons died, and the remaining ones moved to Tamriel.

Blood Dragon

Blood dragons are known for their greenish-gray skin and toothy mouth, as well as small, sharp growths on the end of their tails. The most famous blood dragon was Skakmat, a close friend of the witch Nulfaga.

Frost Dragon

The Frost Dragon has glossy white skin with massive black spikes protruding from its spine.

Elder Dragon

The Elder Dragon has tough bronze skin and is a master of using Yol (fire) or Fo (frost) to breathe deadly air. This species has a very high health pool and is considered one of the strongest.

Ancient Dragon

Ancient dragons are characterized by their reddish skin with strange black markings and spikes. They, like the Elders, are among the strongest dragons that use Yol (fire) or Fo (frost) in their breath.

Skeletal-Dragon

This is not really a dragon, but a dragon skeleton resurrected by magic. Although it does not fly and does not have a dragon soul, it is still very dangerous in close combat and has a frost breath.
